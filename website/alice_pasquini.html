Mural of Alice Pasquini in Via del Pratello

Author: Alice Pasquini
Place: Via del Pratello, 69
Date: 2018
Current State: Visible
Identifier: streetart:bo:work7
Theme Identity
❯

DESCRIPTION

Alice depicted a girl with a bunch of colourful flowers in her hand, hiding part of her face behind the curtain, recalling that street art “is an art that stirs up controversy” and during her career there has certainly been no shortage of problems, but acknowledging that after all “it is the risk of signing one's name”.
