DESCRIPTION

This untitled mural was created in 2018 by Italian street artists Dado (Alessandro Ferri) and Mambo (Elvis Mambo Pregnolato). It is located on the exterior wall of the CISL (Italian Confederation of Workers' Trade Unions) headquarters on Via Milazzo in Bologna.

The large-scale mural—approximately 50 meters wide and 12 meters high—uses red and green as dominant colors, echoing the identity of the union.

The artwork features two women wearing safety helmets and a man with a mask and helmet, symbolizing labor and highlighting the importance of workers’ safety. Blending abstract style with figurative elements, the mural pays tribute to the working class and reflects the artists' commitment to merging street art with social themes.
