Mural of Etnik in Via del Lavoro 3

Author: Etnik
Place: Via del Lavoro, 3
Date: 2012
Current State: Visible
Identifier: streetart:bo:work11
Theme City
❯

DESCRIPTION

This mural by Etnik represents the precarious balance of the human being and the speed of the contemporary world. Urban agglomerations are broken down and overlapped, and the geometrically extreme architecture becomes the emblem of the concept of an "urban cage".

This vision reflects the artist’s poetics, which see metropolitan centers as places where man is trapped, overwhelmed by the rapid and chaotic rhythms of modern life. Etnik critiques this dynamic using strong, bright, and contrasting colors, creating wall paintings in which the identification of buildings is sometimes almost impossible due to their abstract fragmentation.

Through this visual complexity, the work encourages reflection on urban alienation and the disorientation of modern existence, presenting a stylized cityscape that becomes both captivating and unsettling.
