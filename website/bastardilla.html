Mural of Bastardilla in Via Tolmino 9

Author: Bastardilla
Place: Via Tolomino, 9
Date: 2014
Current State: Visible
Identifier: streetart:bo:work9
Theme Social Theme
❯

DESCRIPTION

This untitled mural was created by Colombian street artist Bastardilla as part of the FRONTIER street art project. It features a powerful and poetic scene: a circle of "matchstick children" sitting together, collectively igniting a flame. This symbolic flame represents the spread of knowledge and the transformative power of collective wisdom.

The "matchstick children" evoke the fragility and the incredible potential of pre-adolescent thought, highlighting the importance of community, learning, and the shared spark that ignites growth. The work celebrates the awakening of young minds, portraying education and solidarity as vital forces capable of lighting up the future.

Through this mural, Bastardilla captures a delicate balance between innocence and strength, emphasizing the value of nurturing curiosity and collective intelligence in youth.
